Air Freight

A fully flexible unaccompanied air service with an immediate pick-up time and door-to-door dedicated services. This is offered via the next special commercial or network flight , within the shortest period of time. Globally available, next flight service through door-to-door within two hours to three hours of order, One transparent door-to-door price, End-to-end proactive tracking and monitoring Online service, Customs clearance/document preparation ,Dangerous goods and temperature-controlled handling Our additional ‘secure’ service level meets high security and emergency needs, with an accompanied door-to-door pick-up and delivery, provided by an onboard courier or safety chartered aircraft.
